Saviors Of Starvation

All hotels have perfectly good food that they cannot sell at the end of the day. Large amount of these food goes wasted and is thrown away in the dumping zone. In India many people die due to starvation and many NGOs are doing the job providing food to the needy, but they do it manually, which cannot be efficient. We provided a solution which connects all these segments, and makes the job easier, flexible and efficient. With this platform not only, food banks can serve more hungry people additionally restaurants will also have a meaningful channel to distribute or dispose of the surplus food. Where business can contribute to a sustainable environment in a meaningful way at the same time charities help fight food poverty. This platform helps decrease hunger in society and also prevents a lot of food from being wasted.
